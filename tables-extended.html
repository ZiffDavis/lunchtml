Tables — more tags…

<thead>, <tbody>, <tfoot> & <th>

HTML:

        <table border="1">
            <thead>
                <tr>
                    <th>head 1, cell 1</th>
                    <th>head 1, cell 2</th>
                </tr>
            </thead>
            <tbody>
                <tr>
                    <td>row 1, cell 1</td>
                    <td>row 1, cell 2</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>row 2, cell 1</td>
                    <td>row 2, cell 2</td>
                </tr>
            </tbody>
            <tfoot>
                <tr>
                    <td colspan="2">footer</td>
                </tr>
            </tfoot>
        </table>

RESULT:

head 1, cell 1 head 1, cell 2
row 1, cell 1 row 1, cell 2
row 2, cell 1 row 2, cell 2
footer
